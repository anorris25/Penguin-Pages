My Book Clubs

Classics with a Twist

2 books

This book club is for readers who enjoy well-known classics and their parodic counterparts. Our first pairing of this book club will be Jane Austen's, Pride and Prejudice, and Seth Grahame-Smith's Pride and Prejudice and Zombies, a novel that subverts the classic 'enemies to lovers' trope into a love triangle interrupted by the living dead.

Pittsburgh Non-fiction

2 books

This book club explores books about the city of Pittsburgh and its rich culture and history, for yinzers by the yinzers. Our first novel of this book club will be Colter Harper's, Jazz in the Hill: Nightlife and Narratives of A Pittsburgh Neighborhood. This book is for the readers interested in the lesser-known stories of Pittsburgh's past, with an emphasis on authors who originate or have a personal connection to the city.
