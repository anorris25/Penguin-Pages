We are a book club based in Pittsburgh, PA that focuses on connecting adult readers living in the city that are looking for a community of readers with shared literary interests. The website is open to all users interested, but there is a focus on the Pittsburgh reading community.


Go to the Home page, find a club that interests you, and click the "Join" button. It will then appear on your My Book Clubs page.


Our book clubs meet bi-weekly in our discussion forums, with a week off in between books.


Our platform is meant to be enjoyed fully online. As of now, there are specific discussion forums for each book being read. In the future, we plan to hold bi-weekly Zoom meetings to discuss questions provided for each section of the book being read.
