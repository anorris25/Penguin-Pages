Classics with a Twist

Book 1

Pride and Prejudice

by Jane Austen

In the elegant countryside of early 19th-century England, spirited Elizabeth Bennet navigates society’s expectations, family pressures, and unexpected encounters with the proud but intriguing Mr. Darcy. Witty, sharp, and full of charm, Pride and Prejudice is a timeless exploration of love, reputation, and the quiet rebellions of a woman determined to think for herself.

Book 2

Pride and Prejudice and Zombies

by Seth Grahame-Smith

This wildly inventive twist on a literary classic blends Regency-era romance with a deadly outbreak of the undead. Elizabeth Bennet is not only clever and independent — she's also a fierce zombie slayer. As she crosses swords (and wits) with the brooding Mr. Darcy, England's polite society is forced to confront an unexpected invasion. Pride and Prejudice and Zombies is a hilarious, action-packed mashup where manners meet mayhem.
